FAQ

Questions?
Answered.

Does it work with any YouTube video? +
Yes — paste any public YouTube URL or upload a video file directly (MP4, MOV, AVI up to 500MB). We fetch the transcript automatically for AI clip detection.
How is VidPost different from Opus Clip? +
Opus Clip only does video clips. VidPost also generates LinkedIn posts, Twitter threads, Instagram captions, TikTok scripts, and YouTube Shorts scripts from the same video — and auto-posts all of them. Plus our AI learns from your analytics to improve over time.
Is my content safe and private? +
Yes. Uploaded videos are deleted from our servers within 1 hour of processing. We never store your video content permanently. Your OAuth tokens are encrypted in our database and never shared with third parties.
Which platforms can I auto-post to? +
Currently live: LinkedIn, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and TikTok. X/Twitter and Snapchat are coming soon. Connect accounts in the "Connect Accounts" tab.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — cancel anytime with one click, no questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Who built this? +
VidPost AI was built by a 15-year-old developer from Jodhpur, India. It started as a weekend project and grew into a full product. We're a small team obsessed with helping creators spend less time on distribution and more time creating.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

VidPost AI ("we", "us", "our") operates vidpost-ai.vercel.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and password when you create an account.
  • OAuth tokens: Access tokens from LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok when you connect your accounts. These are encrypted and stored securely.
  • Video content: Videos you upload for processing. These are deleted from our servers within 1 hour of processing completion.
  • Usage data: Information about how you use our service, including clips created and posts published.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide clip generation and auto-posting services
  • To improve our AI models and clip detection accuracy
  • To send service-related emails (no marketing without consent)
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security enabled. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest. Video files are stored temporarily on Railway servers and deleted within 1 hour.

Third-Party Services

We use: Supabase (database), Railway (backend hosting), Vercel (frontend hosting), Groq (AI generation), Supadata (transcript fetching). Each has their own privacy policy.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing hello@vidpostai.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

OAuth Permissions

We request only the minimum permissions needed to post content on your behalf. We never read your private messages, contacts, or personal data from connected platforms.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at hello@vidpostai.com
