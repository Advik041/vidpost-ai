FAQ

Does it work with any YouTube video? +
Yes — paste any public YouTube URL or upload a video file directly (MP4, MOV, AVI up to 500MB). We automatically fetch the transcript for AI clip detection and post generation.
What makes VidPost AI unique? +
VidPost AI is the only platform that combines AI video clipping with full platform-native text post generation — LinkedIn posts, X threads, Instagram captions, TikTok scripts — all from the same video. We also support X/Twitter and Facebook, include a full studio editor with B-Roll, filler removal, brand kit and AI thumbnails, and auto-post to all 6 platforms from one dashboard.
Why is my YouTube URL not loading? +
If a YouTube URL fails to load in the editor, try uploading the video file directly using the "Upload file" tab in the Clips section. This bypasses any regional restrictions and is often faster. MP4/MOV files up to 500MB are supported.
Is my content safe and private? +
Yes. Uploaded videos are permanently deleted from our servers within 1 hour of processing. We never store your video content long-term. OAuth tokens are encrypted and never shared with third parties.
Which platforms can I auto-post to? +
All 6 are live: LinkedIn, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, X/Twitter and Facebook Pages. Connect accounts in the "Connect Accounts" tab inside the dashboard.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — one click, no questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period. We'll never charge you after cancellation.
Who built VidPost AI? +
VidPost AI was built by a 15-year-old developer from Jodhpur, India. What started as a weekend project grew into a full product used by 2,000+ creators. We're obsessed with helping creators spend less time distributing and more time creating.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2025 · Effective immediately

VidPost AI ("Company", "we", "us", "our") is operated by an individual developer based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. We operate the website vidpost-ai.vercel.app and related services (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service. Please read this carefully. If you disagree, please discontinue use.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We never store plaintext passwords — authentication is handled by Supabase with industry-standard encryption.

OAuth Tokens: When you connect social media accounts (LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, X/Twitter, Facebook), we store encrypted OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens. These are stored with AES-256 encryption in our Supabase database. We request only the minimum permissions necessary to post content on your behalf.

Video Content: Videos you upload or process via YouTube URL are temporarily stored on Railway servers for processing. All source video files are automatically and permanently deleted within 1 hour of processing completion. Generated clips are retained for up to 24 hours for download, then deleted.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the Service, including: clips created, posts published, platforms connected, feature usage, and error logs. This data is used to improve the Service and is never sold.

Payment Information: Billing is handled entirely by Stripe, Inc. We never see, store, or process your payment card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to all payment data.

Log Data: Our servers automatically record: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of access, and referring URL. This is standard server log data used for security and debugging.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • To process video clips and generate AI content on your behalf
  • To post content to connected social media platforms when you instruct us to
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, billing receipts)
  • To improve our AI models and clip detection algorithms using anonymised aggregate data
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations

We will never send you marketing emails without your explicit consent. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) servers in the United States with row-level security (RLS) enabled — meaning each user can only access their own data. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest. All data transmission uses TLS 1.3 encryption.

We implement reasonable technical and organisational security measures. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase (database and authentication) — supabase.com/privacy
  • Railway (backend hosting) — railway.app/privacy
  • Vercel (frontend hosting) — vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Stripe (payment processing) — stripe.com/privacy
  • Groq (AI text generation) — groq.com/privacy
  • Supadata (YouTube transcript fetching) — supadata.ai/privacy

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Video files are deleted within 1 hour. Generated clips are deleted within 24 hours. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time (see Section 7). We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. OAuth Permissions — What We Access

We request only the permissions necessary to post content on your behalf. We do not access your private messages, follower lists, or personal profile data from connected platforms. Specific permissions requested:

  • LinkedIn: w_member_social (post on your behalf)
  • YouTube: youtube.upload (upload videos)
  • Instagram / Facebook: pages_manage_posts, publish_video
  • TikTok: video.publish
  • X/Twitter: tweet.write, media.write

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, withdraw consent at any time, and lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority. To exercise any right, email hello@vidpostai.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.
