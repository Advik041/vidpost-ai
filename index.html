FAQ

Common questions.

Does it work with any YouTube video?+
Yes — paste any public YouTube URL. For age-restricted or private videos, use the Upload tab to upload directly from your computer.
How accurate is the AI clip detection?+
Our Groq-powered model reads the full transcript and scores segments on hook strength, emotional tone, and pacing. You get 5 ranked clips with virality scores — then you pick and fine-tune in the editor.
Can I upload my own videos instead of YouTube links?+
Yes — use the Upload tab to upload any MP4, MOV, or WebM up to 500MB directly from your device.
What platforms does auto-posting support?+
LinkedIn, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, X (Twitter), and Facebook Pages. Connect once via OAuth and post to all of them in a single click.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes — one click in Manage Subscription. No questions asked. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
