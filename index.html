FAQ
Questions?
Real answers.
How is VidPost different from Opus Clip? +
Opus Clip only does video clips — and charges by the minute. VidPost does video clips PLUS text posts for LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, TikTok and YouTube Shorts, all from the same video. Plus auto-posting to 7 platforms, smart scheduling, and an analytics loop that makes your clips better over time. Flat monthly fee, no credit traps.
Does it work with any YouTube video? +
Yes — paste any public YouTube URL or upload a video file directly (MP4, MOV, AVI up to 500MB). We fetch the transcript automatically for AI clip detection.
Is there really no clip limit? +
Yes. Unlike Opus Clip which charges 1 credit per minute of video (so a 60-minute podcast costs 60 credits), VidPost charges a flat monthly fee. Process 1 video or 1,000 — same price.
Is my content private and secure? +
Yes. Uploaded videos are deleted from our servers within 1 hour of processing. We never store your video content permanently. OAuth tokens are encrypted in our database using industry-standard encryption.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, cancel anytime with one click. Unlike Opus Clip (which has documented difficulty removing payment methods), we make cancellation instant. No questions asked. 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.
Who built this? +
VidPost AI was built by a 15-year-old developer from Jodhpur, India. What started as a weekend project grew into a product after frustration with Opus Clip's pricing and limitations. We're a lean team obsessed with helping creators spend less time on distribution.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

VidPost AI ("we", "us") operates vidpost-ai.vercel.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What we collect

  • Account: Email and password when you sign up.
  • OAuth tokens: Access tokens from connected platforms. Encrypted at rest. Never shared.
  • Video content: Files you upload. Deleted within 1 hour of processing.
  • Usage data: Clips created, posts published, analytics pulled from platforms.

How we use it

  • To provide clip generation and auto-posting
  • To improve AI clip scoring over time

Data storage

All data stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security. OAuth tokens encrypted at rest. Video files stored temporarily on Railway and deleted within 1 hour. We never sell your data.
